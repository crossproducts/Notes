# Platform Engineering

> Building an internal developer platform — golden paths, self-service environments,
> and developer portals on top of the lower DevOps layers.

## Why it matters (2026)

DevOps is shifting from "every team writes its own YAML and Terraform" to **internal
developer platforms**: golden paths, paved roads, and self-service infrastructure.
This is one of the biggest skill gaps to close for 2026 platform roles.

- [ ] Internal Developer Platform (IDP) — golden paths, paved roads
